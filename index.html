ABOUT THE COMPANY

Inquisit Consultants LLP is a multidisciplinary professional service firm providing a wide range of sustainable solutions and advisory support to address such challenges faced by individuals and businesses. Each of the partner of Inquisit Consultants bring along years of multi-sectoral and multi-functional experience. We are a competent organization in providing technical assistance to clusters as the partners have experience of project implementation, both as Nodal Agency (NA) and Technical Agency (TA) for the SFURTI scheme of Ministry of MSME, Government of India. The partners got approval and implemented over 20 projects as Nodal Agency (worth INR 68.4 Crore) and 19 as Technical Agency (worth INR 76.72 Crore), from agriculture and allied, horticulture, food processing, NonTimber Forest Products, and services sectors which are currently active in 12 different states of India .

We believe, developing a sustainable cluster demand making long term linkages between different stakeholders such as corporates, foundations, government institutions, civil societies, multilateral/bilateral agencies & impact funds, across diverse industry as per the requirement of the specific cluster. This approach to development of clusters has immensely benefited the projects when they were under the management of partners of Inquisit Consultants.
